In-Situ's UX Writing Guidelines

Consistent voice is important to In-Situ. When users read text in the VuSitu mobile app, they should recognize it as In-Situ's. The same is true of text in other software and documentation.

To achieve this consistency, I created In-Situ's UX writing guidelines. They're based on the five UX principles that guide all the company's hardware and software development.
