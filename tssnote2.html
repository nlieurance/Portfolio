The Results:

I made several changes in order to improve readability.

Better Usability


I broke up the text with short, clear headings. The parallel structure (each heading starts with a verb) brings continuity to the document.

Improved Accessibility


I used plain language, contractions, and short paragraphs to make this document easy to read. In fact, the Hemingway app gives it a readability score of 6th grade. Because In-Situ users come from varied educational backgrounds, it's important to make documentation available to a wide audience. Simple language is also easy to translate.

Greater Usefulness


The new technical note isn't just informative, it's also useful. Step-by-step instructions guide readers through the necessary lab work.
